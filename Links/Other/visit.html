Our gym etiquette

  1. Always respect your training partners
  2. We have zero tolerance to trying to hurt others
  3. Be friendly
  4. Listen to your coaches
  5. Leave egos at the door
  6. All sizes, colours, sexes, and religions are treated equally
  7. Bullying is not tolerated
  8. Look after your hygiene (we have complimentary deodorant)
  9. Wipe down equipment after using it
  10. Replace your equipment after use
  11. Take care of the gym
  12. If you aren’t sure, ask one of our friendly staff
Ready to sign up?

Know what you want to work on? All first time visitors can now book in for a class online. We only have limited spots per class so be sure to secure your spot today!
